What it models

Everything that shapes your retirement

RetirementIQ models the full complexity of retirement finances — the same calculations a good advisor would run, available to you directly.

📊
Monte Carlo Simulation
2,000 randomized market scenarios. Understand your real probability of success — not just the optimistic average case.

Roth Conversion Optimizer

Social Security Modeling
