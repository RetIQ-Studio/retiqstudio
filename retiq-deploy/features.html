RetIQ Features

Comprehensive retirement planning that runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no cloud, no subscriptions.

📊 Core Planning Engine

Core Year-by-Year Projection

Full financial projection from current age through end of plan. Tracks earned income, savings contributions, investment growth, expenses, withdrawals, taxes, and net worth every year individually.

Core Multi-Account Modeling

Separate tracking for Pre-Tax (401k/IRA), Roth, and Taxable accounts. Contribution limits enforced per SECURE 2.0 including catch-up provisions for ages 50+ and the enhanced 60-63 window.

Core Spouse Integration

Full dual-income household modeling. Independent retirement ages, income trajectories, savings rates, SS benefits, pensions, and SSDI flowing into a unified household projection.

Core Inflation-Adjusted View

Toggle between future dollars and today's purchasing power. All charts and tables update in real time so you can evaluate plans in dollars that feel meaningful.

🏛 Tax & RMD Engine

Tax Federal & State Tax

2025 MFJ federal brackets applied year by year. Configurable state tax rate. Taxable income computed from earned income, Social Security (up to 85% taxable), RMDs, Roth conversions, pensions, and capital gains.

Tax RMD Calculations (SECURE 2.0)

Required Minimum Distributions using the Uniform Lifetime Table with SECURE Act 2.0 start ages (73-75 depending on birth year). RMDs sourced from pre-tax accounts and fully included in taxable income.

Tax Roth Conversion Optimizer

Tests hundreds of strategies varying amounts, bracket targets (fill 12%/22%/24%), and timing windows. Ranks top 8 by composite score minimizing lifetime taxes, RMDs, and IRMAA. Side-by-side tax burden and account composition charts.

Tax Charitable Giving & QCD

Qualified Charitable Distributions up to $108,000/year (2025 IRS limit) directly from IRAs to satisfy RMDs tax-free. Models both QCD and standard charitable deductions with their distinct tax impacts.

🏥 Social Security & Healthcare

SS Social Security Optimization

SSA-precise benefit calculations using exact monthly reduction and credit rates (5/9% and 5/12% early, 2/3% delayed). FRA in integer months per birth year. Claiming comparison tables 62-70 with breakeven analysis. Manual SSA statement entry supported.

SS SSDI Modeling

Disability Insurance bridge with automatic conversion to retirement benefits at FRA. Independent SSDI for both spouses with taxable income integration and post-SSDI claiming comparison tables.

Health Medicare & IRMAA

2025 IRMAA brackets modeled year by year using 2-year MAGI lookback. Medicare premiums inflate at configurable medical cost inflation. Income-related surcharges from Roth conversions tracked explicitly.

Health Pre-Medicare Healthcare

If retiring before 65, models ACA marketplace or COBRA costs during the coverage gap. Configurable annual cost and medical inflation rate with cumulative gap cost summary.

🎯 Planning & Analysis

Plan Monte Carlo Simulation

2,000 randomized scenarios with year-by-year market and inflation variation. Reports success probability, percentile outcomes (10th/25th/50th/75th/90th), and worst-case analysis with configurable volatility.

Plan Scenario Comparison

16 built-in scenarios including single-variable and combination tests ("Low Return + High Inflation," "Early Retire + Lower Return," "+20% Expenses + High Inflation"). Multi-category chart comparison.

Plan Survivor Analysis

Spousal death at configurable ages with survivor SS benefit recalculation, expense adjustment (configurable survivor ratio), and automatic filing status change from MFJ to single brackets.

Plan Legacy & Estate Goals

Set a target inheritance amount and track progress. The projection shows whether your plan achieves the legacy goal at end-of-plan across base and Monte Carlo scenarios.

💾 Data & Export

Export HTML Snapshot

Export your entire plan as a self-contained HTML file with charts, tables, and all inputs that opens in any browser without the app. Perfect for archiving or sharing with an advisor.

Export CSV Projection Export

Download the full projection table as CSV for analysis in Excel or Google Sheets. Every column available for your own modeling.

App PWA — Install & Offline

Install RetIQ as a standalone app on desktop or mobile. Works offline after installation. All data stays on your device.

App Privacy by Design

Zero telemetry. No accounts. No cloud sync. Your financial data never leaves your browser. The entire application runs client-side JavaScript with no backend dependencies beyond payment processing.

📋 Additional Inputs

Income Pension / Annuity

Model defined-benefit pensions, military retirement, or annuities for both spouses. Configurable start age and shared COLA rate (0% for fixed, 2-3% for inflation-adjusted).

Income Expense Modes

Fixed-amount or percentage-of-portfolio withdrawal modes. Inflation-adjusted expense growth with configurable survivor expense ratio for single-income periods.

Plan Debt / Liabilities

Track mortgages, auto loans, student loans with payoff schedules. Monthly payment, interest rate, and remaining balance modeled through payoff date.

UI Accessibility

Colorblind-friendly palette, dark and light themes, responsive layout for mobile and desktop, monospace projection tables, and interactive chart hover tooltips.
