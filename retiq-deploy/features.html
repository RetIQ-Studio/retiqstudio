RetIQ Features

Comprehensive retirement planning that runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no cloud, no subscriptions.

📊 Core Planning Engine

Core Year-by-Year Projection

A full financial projection from your current age through end of plan. Every year is modeled individually — income, savings, investment growth, expenses, withdrawals, taxes, and net worth — so you can see exactly when and why things change.

Core Multi-Account Modeling

Track Pre-Tax (401k, 403b, 457b, IRA), Roth, and Taxable accounts separately. Each account carries its own expected return rate — great for modeling a conservative bond allocation alongside an aggressive stock portfolio. Quick Add presets let you add common account types (HSA, HYSA, Brokerage, 529, and more) with one click.

Core Spouse Integration

Full dual-income household modeling. Independent retirement ages, income trajectories, savings rates, SS benefits, pensions, and SSDI — all flowing into a unified household projection. Includes spouse-specific account presets.

Core Inflation-Adjusted View

Toggle between future dollars and today's purchasing power with a single click. All charts and tables update instantly so you can evaluate your plan in dollars that feel real and meaningful to you right now.

🏛 Tax & RMD Engine

Tax Federal & State Tax

2025 federal tax brackets (MFJ and single) applied year by year. State taxes modeled using a dropdown of all 50 states and DC with appropriate rates — just select your state. Taxable income computed from earned income, Social Security (up to 85% taxable), RMDs, Roth conversions, pensions, and capital gains.

Tax RMD Calculations (SECURE 2.0)

Required Minimum Distributions using the Uniform Lifetime Table with SECURE Act 2.0 start ages (73–75 depending on birth year). RMDs come from pre-tax accounts and flow into taxable income. The dashboard shows your RMD start age and peak annual RMD so there are no surprises.

Tax Roth Conversion Optimizer

Tests ~200+ strategies varying fixed amounts, bracket targets (fill 12%/22%/24%), Smart Fill (IRMAA-aware bracket fill), and timing windows. Ranks the top 8 by a composite score that weighs tax savings, IRMAA impact, and net worth gain. Apply any result with one click — it becomes your active strategy across all tabs.

Tax Roth Conversion Strategies

Six strategy types to match how you actually think about conversions: Fixed Amount, Fill Tax Bracket, Smart Fill (IRMAA-Aware), Target MAGI Ceiling, % of Pre-Tax Balance, and Custom Schedule. Each shows only its relevant controls. Impact Analysis gives an instant before/after comparison with a Conversion Advantage chart showing where the strategy breaks even.

🏥 Social Security & Healthcare

SS Social Security Optimization

SSA-precise benefit calculations using exact monthly reduction and credit rates (5/9% and 5/12% early, 2/3% delayed per month). FRA computed in integer months per birth year. Side-by-side claiming comparison tables for ages 62–70 with breakeven analysis. Enter your own SSA statement amount for maximum accuracy.

SS SSDI Modeling

Social Security Disability Insurance bridge with automatic conversion to retirement benefits at FRA. Independent SSDI for both spouses with taxable income integration and post-SSDI claiming comparison tables.

Health Medicare & IRMAA

2025 IRMAA brackets modeled year by year using the 2-year MAGI lookback. Medicare premiums inflate at configurable medical cost inflation. Income-related surcharges from Roth conversions are tracked explicitly so you can see the real cost of aggressive conversions.

Health Pre-Medicare Healthcare

If you retire before 65, RetIQ models the healthcare gap — ACA marketplace, COBRA, or private insurance costs. Configurable annual cost and medical inflation rate with a clear cumulative gap cost summary.

🎯 Planning & Analysis

Plan Monte Carlo Simulation

2,000 randomized scenarios with year-by-year market and inflation variation. Reports your success probability, percentile outcomes (10th/25th/50th/75th/90th), and worst-case analysis. Configurable volatility so you can stress-test conservative or aggressive assumptions.

Plan Scenario Comparison

16+ built-in scenarios including single-variable tests and realistic combinations like "Low Return + High Inflation," "Early Retire + Lower Return," and "Higher Pre-Ret Expenses (+20%)." Multi-category chart lets you compare any metric across all scenarios at once.

Plan Strategy Report Card

Automatically tests 7–9 alternative strategies against your current plan and ranks them by your chosen goal: Max Net Worth, Min Lifetime Taxes, Min Tax + IRMAA, or Max SS Income. Compares SS claim timing (62, FRA, 70), Roth conversion approaches (Smart Fill 22%, 24%, none), combined SS + Roth strategies, and delayed retirement. One-click Apply to adopt any strategy. SSDI-aware — suppresses inapplicable alternatives automatically.

Plan Survivor Analysis

Model what happens when either spouse passes at a configurable age. Expenses adjust by a survivor ratio, filing status switches from MFJ to single, and survivor SS benefits are calculated per SSA rules — including the higher-of-own-or-deceased benefit logic.

Plan Legacy & Estate Goals

Set a target inheritance amount and track your progress toward it. The dashboard and projection show whether your plan meets the legacy goal — and by how much — across base and Monte Carlo scenarios.

💰 Expenses & Cash Flow

Expenses Pre-Retirement & Post-Retirement

Separate expense inputs for working years and retirement with independent inflation rates. Pre-retirement expenses flow through full cash flow modeling (income minus expenses, savings, and taxes) with automatic shortfall detection. Post-retirement supports fixed-amount or percentage-of-portfolio withdrawal modes.

Expenses Retirement Expense Phases

Spending patterns change through retirement. Model the common pattern: full spending in active early retirement, reduced spending in your mid-70s, then increased costs in your 80s+ as healthcare needs grow. Fully configurable age boundaries and spending percentages.

Health Long-Term Care

70% of people over 65 will need some form of long-term care. Model annual LTC costs (nursing home, assisted living, or home aide), start age, duration, and LTC-specific inflation. If you have LTC insurance, enter your annual coverage and see the net out-of-pocket impact.

Plan Liabilities & Debts

Track mortgages, alimony, student loans, and other recurring obligations. Each liability has a type, monthly payment, active age range, and tax-deductible flag. Payments are added to expenses during the active period and disappear after payoff.

💖 Charitable & Income

Charity Charitable Giving & QCD

Model charitable giving with Qualified Charitable Distributions (QCDs) from IRAs after age 70½. QCDs count toward RMDs without adding to taxable income — a powerful tax strategy. Supports simple fixed annual giving or a flexible multi-phase schedule with age ranges and percentage-of-income options.

Income Pension / Annuity

Model defined-benefit pensions, military retirement, or annuities for both spouses independently. Configurable start age and shared COLA rate (0% for fixed pensions, 2–3% for inflation-adjusted). Pension income flows into taxable income each year it is received.

Income Contribution Limits

When enabled, savings respect annual 401(k) and IRA limits ($23,500 + catch-up in 2025) with age 50+ catch-up and SECURE 2.0 age 60–63 super catch-up provisions. Any savings beyond the limits overflow to taxable accounts automatically.

Income Other Retirement Income

Model additional income streams that begin at retirement — rental income, part-time work, or annuity payments — separate from pensions and Social Security.

💾 Data, Export & Privacy

Export HTML Snapshot

Export your entire plan as a self-contained HTML file with all inputs, charts, and projections. It opens in any browser without the app — perfect for archiving, printing, or sharing with a financial advisor.

Export CSV Projection Export

Download the full year-by-year projection table as CSV for your own analysis in Excel or Google Sheets. Every column — income, taxes, withdrawals, account balances, RMDs, IRMAA — is included.

App Projection Charts

Interactive charts with selectable categories — choose up to 3 at once from net worth, income, expenses, federal tax, state tax, withdrawals, RMDs, Roth conversions, IRMAA, pensions, healthcare, liabilities, LTC, charity, and individual account balances. Toggle the data table on and off.

App PWA — Install & Offline

Install RetIQ as a standalone app on desktop or mobile. Works offline after installation. All data stays on your device and auto-saves between sessions.

App Privacy by Design

Zero telemetry. No accounts. No cloud sync. Your financial data never leaves your browser. The entire application runs client-side JavaScript with no backend dependencies beyond payment processing.

App Share RetIQ

A built-in share button uses your device's native share sheet (on mobile) or copies a link to your clipboard (on desktop). Help someone else plan their retirement — it's one click away.

🎨 Design & Accessibility

UI Dark & Light Themes

Switch between dark and light themes with a single click. Your preference is saved automatically. Both themes are designed for extended reading of financial data.

UI Accessibility

Colorblind-friendly palette, responsive layout for mobile and desktop, interactive chart hover tooltips, and monospace projection tables for easy number scanning.
