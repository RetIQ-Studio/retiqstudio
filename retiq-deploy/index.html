What it models

Everything that shapes your retirement

RetirementIQ models the full complexity of retirement finances — the same calculations a good advisor would run, available to you directly.

📊
Monte Carlo Simulation
Three simulation models — Normal, Historical Bootstrap (1928–2024), and Stress Test — with up to 2,000 scenarios each. Understand your real probability of success across gentle, realistic, and extreme market conditions.
🔄
Roth Conversion Ladder
Hundreds of strategies ranked by lifetime taxes saved, RMD reduction, and net worth impact. Built-in 5-Year Rule Tracker shows when each conversion tranche becomes penalty-free.
🏛️
Social Security Modeling
Claim timing from 62 to 70, spousal and survivor benefits, breakeven analysis, SSDI integration, and retirement earnings test for early claimers who keep working.
💊
Medicare & Healthcare
Current IRMAA surcharge brackets modeled year by year. Pre-Medicare gap with six source options. ACA Marketplace subsidy based on projected MAGI. Long-term care with insurance inflation protection.
📋
RMD Calculations
SECURE 2.0 compliant, using the IRS Uniform Lifetime Table. Know your RMD start age and annual amounts years in advance.
🎯
Scenario & Stress Test
10 named scenarios plus an allocation-aware bear market stress test. See the long-term impact of any decision — or a sudden market crash — immediately.
🏦
5-Account Modeling
Pre-tax, Roth, HSA, brokerage, and cash/HYS — each with independent return rates, tax treatment, and withdrawal order. Your actual account mix, modeled precisely.
🕊️
Survivor Planning
Model what happens when either spouse passes — expense adjustments, filing status change, survivor SS benefits per SSA rules, pension survivor benefits, and life insurance death benefit deposits.
💾
Export & Save
A complete offline HTML snapshot of your plan, or projection tables as CSV for Excel or Sheets. Your data, your way.
🏆
Strategy Report Card
Auto-tests 7–9 alternative strategies and ranks them by your goal — max net worth, min taxes, or max Social Security. One click to apply any alternative.
🔎
Maximum Spending
Find the highest sustainable retirement spending that keeps your money lasting. Full-engine binary search across taxes, RMDs, IRMAA, and every other factor.
📅
This Year's Plan
A printable summary of your current year — income, withdrawals, Roth conversions, taxes, and expenses in one card. Print or save as PDF with one click.
🎚️
Simple / Full Mode
Two UI modes, one engine. Simple gives you 7 streamlined tabs for quick planning. Full reveals all 14 tabs with every advanced feature. Toggle anytime — your numbers never change.
📋
Example Plans
Three pre-built families spanning simple to complex — Social Security reliance, Roth optimization with HSA, and full-complexity with pension, estate tax, and LTC. See what’s possible before entering your own numbers.
🏠
One-Time Cash Events
Model home sales, rental property liquidation, or other large one-time inflows with capital gains, cost basis, and Section 121 exclusions.
🛡️
Life Insurance
Term and permanent policies with premiums in expenses and tax-free death benefits (IRC §101) that deposit into survivor accounts. Integrates with legacy composition.
🏛️
State Income Tax
Progressive brackets for all 50 states + DC. Social Security exemptions, retirement income exclusions, and age-gated deductions modeled per state.

What RetirementIQ is — and isn't

✓
A powerful financial modeling tool — showing you how your retirement savings are likely to behave across a wide range of market conditions, tax scenarios, and spending decisions.
✓
Fully offline after loading. Works without an internet connection. Export your plan and it works anywhere, on any device, forever.
✓

Not a crystal ball. Markets are unpredictable. The Monte Carlo simulation shows a range of outcomes — including difficult ones — so you can plan with realistic expectations, not false certainty.
